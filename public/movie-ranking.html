The Best Movies According to Vicky

My Top 4 Movies of ALL-TIME!

My Cousin Vinny

They simply don't make movies like this anymore. The jokes still hit the same - from when I watched it the first time.

Uncle Buck

I usually watch this around the holidays. Again, another funny movie that wouldn't be the same if it was made today.
